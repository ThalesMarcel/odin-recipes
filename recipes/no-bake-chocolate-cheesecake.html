No Bake Chocolate Cheesecake

Description

This no-bake chocolate cheesecake is deliciously rich but easy to make! It's lovely topped with some sweetened whipped cream, fresh berries, or a raspberry coulis.

Original recipe available on Allrecipes.

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Additional Time: 1 hr 30 mins
  • Total Time: 2 hrs
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 1 cheesecake of 20 centimeters

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup salted butter, melted
  • ½ cup white sugar, divided
  • 140 grams dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • 2 or 3 tablespoons hot water, or as needed
  • 225 grams cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and 1 tablespoon of sugar; press crumb mixture into bottom and 1 centimeter up the sides of an 20 centimeters springform pan. Refrigerate crust while you make the filling.
  2. Melt chocolate in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl in the microwave on high power for 1 to 2 minutes, or until melted, stirring every 15 seconds; set aside to cool.
  3. Whisk cocoa powder and enough warm water together in a medium bowl to form a smooth paste.
  4. Beat cream cheese and remaining sugar into cocoa mixture until combined; set aside.
  5. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until soft peaks form. Fold in cooled melted chocolate.
  6. Fold chocolate cream mixture into cream cheese mixture until thoroughly combined; spread on top of graham cracker crust.
  7. Freeze cheesecake for 1 hour. Thaw in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • Taste a little of the filling to ensure that the right amount of chocolaty goodness has been reached. You can add a little more of the cocoa powder and water mixture to taste.
  • Instead of freezing, the cheesecake can also be covered and chilled at least 2 hours before serving.
  • Cheesecake may be stored in an airtight container and chilled up to 5 days.
