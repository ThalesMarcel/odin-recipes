Waldorf Salad

Description

Waldorf salad is delicious, and you can vary the ingredients in this recipe to your preference. Try adding diced, roasted chicken to make this salad a meal!

Recipe by Penny, originally available on Allrecipes.

  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples - peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt together in a serving bowl.
  2. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
